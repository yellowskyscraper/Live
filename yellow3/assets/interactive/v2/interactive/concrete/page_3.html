 Concrete Destination:
 is a phrase that expresses the belief that our developed world is destined to expand far beyond the capacity of the land itself; it has also been used to advocate for or justify other development plans. Advocates of Concrete Destination believed that expansion was not only good, but that it was obvious ("Concrete") and certain ("Destination"). Concrete Destination was an explanation or justification for the expansion and developing of our natural world, or, in some interpretations, an ideology or doctrine which helped to promote the process.
