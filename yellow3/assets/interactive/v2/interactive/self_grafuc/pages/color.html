The JIM Corporate Colors.
Color Specifications:
The JIM graphic symbol utilizes none of the vast array of colors available to it. This ensures a prominent and lasting presence of the JIM brand among an increasingly colorful market place. For any reproduction of the graphic symbol itself, the JIM and accompanied assets must remain monochromatic, to ensure integrity in most uncertain applications.

Monochromatic Logo:
The color system includes three, subdued variations, of basic colors. A blue, black and green are available for reproduction. The JIM colors are stand alone, never to be accompanied by a secondary color. Pantone Matching System colors are available for those purposes. When 4-color offset printing is used for printed materials, the mark may be reproduced in the monochromatic 4-color equivalents of the Pantone colors. RGB values are provided for onscreen usage.
