Shanti

Shanti provides practical and emotional support to women with breast cancer. Transportation to and from medical appointments; in-home meal delivery seven days a week; temporary emergency funds; in-home volunteers who provide emotional
and practical support such as grocery shopping, light housekeeping, appointment making; referrals; assistance with applying for financial benefits to cover breast cancer treatments.
