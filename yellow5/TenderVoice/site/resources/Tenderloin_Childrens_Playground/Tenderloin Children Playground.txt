Tenderloin Children's Playground

Operated by San Francisco Recreation and Parks Department, the Tenderloin Children's Playground provides kids and teens a safe and supervised place to play and participate in activities and events. It offers activities for neighborhood youth including pre-school activities, a latch-key program, arts and crafts, dance, sewing, cooking, gardening, woodworking, and year-round sports.
