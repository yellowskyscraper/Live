Coalition on Homelessness

The Coalition on Homelessness unites homeless people and service providers with concerned community members in outreach, advocacy, and community organizing activities to ensure that low-income communities are in a leadership position in the work to promote social justice in San Francisco. COH also publishes the Street Sheet, the second-oldest street newspaper in the United States and currently the most-circulated in the country with 32,000 papers distributed monthly.
