Youth with a Mission

Youth With a Mission works toward the holistic development of individuals, fighting for those who cannot fight for themselves, to bring justice, healing and restoration. Their Tenderloin location offers a variety of services including a drop-in daytime center with games, a food pantry, and a life group for sharing experiences.
